Lumentum is a San Jose–based public company focused on optical and photonic components and subsystems for AI/cloud data centers, telecom, industrial lasers, and sensing (including VCSEL-based 3D sensing). ## What The Company Does

Lumentum describes itself as delivering breakthrough optical and photonic technologies for AI, cloud, telecom, industrial, and sensing applications, with vertically integrated manufacturing and broad materials/semiconductor expertise (including InP). The company traces its lineage to the 2015 spin-off from JDS Uniphase (JDSU), with milestones such as VCSEL volume for consumer 3D sensing (2017), ramp in high-speed optics for hyperscale data centers and 5G-era demand (2019–2021), and the 2023 acquisition of **Cloud Light** to deepen AI/ML-driven connectivity (transceivers/AOCs). Public reporting also segments the business into **Cloud & Networking** vs **Industrial Tech** (and, in some quarters, **Components** vs **Systems**).
